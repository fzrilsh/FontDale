Our Story

A Vision of Curated Excellence

Conceived in 2023 by visionary founder Franz Diego, FontDale was designed from its inception to be more than a mall. It is a living destination. Officially opening its doors in 2025, FontDale has redefined what a premium shopping experience looks and feels like.

Nestled at Arcadia Circle and seamlessly connecting the North Gallery to the South Pavilion through the iconic Lumen Skywalk, FontDale brings together global fashion houses, artisanal dining, and thoughtfully curated lifestyle brands under one breathtaking roof.

What Sets Us Apart

The FontDale Experience

Three defining pillars make FontDale the most anticipated lifestyle destination in the region.

Lumen Skywalk

Our signature glass-and-steel bridge connects North Gallery to South Pavilion, offering a panoramic view that turns every visit into a memorable journey.

Global Fashion

From heritage luxury houses to cutting-edge contemporary labels, FontDale's curated tenant mix brings the world's finest fashion to a single, inspired address.

Diverse Dining

Savor culinary journeys across six continents from Michelin-starred concepts to beloved neighborhood flavors, all within the artfully designed dining pavilions of FontDale.

Events and Culture

A living calendar of art installations, fashion showcases, culinary festivals, and exclusive brand events keeps FontDale vibrant and ever-changing throughout the year.
